I have a magnet in my finger

In December, I heard about a piercing that would let me sense magnetic fields and pick up small, metal objects. In January, my roommate and I ordered small magnets and paid a piercer to put them in our fingers. It was really painful for a few weeks, but almost immediately we realized that we could feel buzzing from our microwave, pulling from magnets, and a barely-perceptible tingle from iron objects. It's been 7 months and I'm still randomly discovering that things are magnetic and being amazed.
